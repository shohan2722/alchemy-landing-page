About Alchemy 43

We built a third option — between clinical and discount

We built Alchemy 43 because we were tired of the med spa experience. We saw a world of extremes: clinical, intimidating offices on one end, and impersonal, discount-driven chains on the other. Neither felt right for people who wanted subtle enhancements without the drama.

So we created a serene, modern retreat staffed by licensed medical providers who are also artists — people who care more about balance than about selling units.

Our founders, like many of our clients, wanted to look refreshed without losing their own expressions. We vowed to build a practice where “I just want to look like me again” was understood completely — where the first conversation always starts with listening, not selling.

Today, with 9 locations and 400+ five-star reviews, we’ve become the go-to for discerning clients across the country. And we still treat every face like a first date: gentle, attentive, and never pushy.
